Didier Drogba

The Greatest African Player of All Time

Here's a timeline of Didier Drogba's life:

  • 1978 - Born in Abidjan, Ivory Coast
  • 1988 - Started playing for Tourcoing
  • 1989 - Started playing for Abbeville
  • 1991 - Started playing for Vannes
  • 1993 - Started playing for Levallois
  • 1997 - Started playing for Le Mans
  • 2002 - Started playing for Guingamp and the Ivory Coast National Team
  • 2003 - Started playing for Marseille
  • 2004 - Started playing for Chelsea
  • 2012 - Started playing for Shanghai Shenhua
  • 2013 - Started playing for Galatasaray
  • 2014 - Started playing for Chelsea and retired from the Ivory Coast National Team
  • 2015 - Started playing for Montreal Impact
  • 2017 - Started playing for Phoenix Rising
